# Research Roadmap

The AXIONYX Intelligence Engine is evolving from a foundational research platform into a mature, multi-application ecosystem. 

## Trajectory

### 1. Research (Complete)
- Formalizing the Constitution.
- Defining the Scientific Information Architecture (SIA).

### 2. Platform Maturity (Current)
- Deployment of the AXIONYX Intelligence Engine (`v1.0.0-core` -> `v1.1.0-canonical`).
- Stabilization of Evidence Passports and API boundaries.

### 3. Application Ecosystem
- **iPhande:** Community-facing visibility and opportunity mapping.
- **SANAS Explorer:** Accredited laboratory interaction and evidence queries.

### 4. Demonstrations
- Complete end-to-end replay audits of specific scientific workflows (e.g., Coal sample certification).
