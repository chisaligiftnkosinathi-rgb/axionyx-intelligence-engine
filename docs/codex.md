# The AXIONYX Codex

The AXIONYX Codex represents the philosophy of stewardship, human oversight, and institutional memory that governs the Intelligence Engine. It separates philosophical governance from the pure technical specification, ensuring that truth remains human-centered and auditable.

## Principles of Stewardship
- **Evidence Over Conclusion:** Conclusions are ephemeral; the evidence that supports them must be permanent and immutable.
- **Transparency & Provenance:** Every claim must be traceable back to its origin.
- **Institutional Memory:** The system must record the context and state of knowledge at any point in time, enabling flawless replay.
- **Human Oversight:** The platform does not make final decisions. It structures relationships and preserves coherence so human stewards can act with clarity.
